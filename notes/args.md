#Word and sentence properties relevant to argument structure#

## Word ##
- Main verb subject and object person, number, gender

	- For object, need to distinguish a) accusative/objective, b) applicative/oblique (ል- ብ-), c) none

- Noun/pronoun person, number, gender

- Noun/pronoun case markers: a) none, b) -ን, c) preposition

	- For modified noun, the case marker appears on the modifier, so maybe the unit should be the noun phrase?

## Sentence ##

- Whether the sentence has an explicit subject and an explicit object

- Identity/position of main verb arguments
	- nsubj, csubj, obj, ccomp, xcomp OR subject and object, grouping different UD relations together

- Identity/position of noun/pronoun head

- Identity/position of main verb subject and object
